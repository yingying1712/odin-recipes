Lemon Drizzle Cake

Descriptions:

A Lemon Drizzle Cake is like a sunny day on a plate!

With its fluffy layers kissed by zesty lemon goodness and a sweet drizzle on top, every bite is a burst of happiness and sunshine ^^

Ingredients:

  • Flour: 2 cups
  • Baking Powder:2 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Softened Butter: 1 cup
  • Sugar: 2 cups
  • Egg: 4 large eggs
  • Lemon: 2 lemons
  • Lemon Juice: 1/2 cup
  • Milk: 2 tablespoons

- Simple Steps to Make Lemon Drizzle Cake -

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch (23cm) round cake pans.
  2. In a bowl, beat softened butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time. Add lemon zest and juice, mix until combined.
  4. In another bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add flour mixture to butter mixture, alternating with milk. Mix until smooth.
  6. Divide batter evenly between pans. Bake for 25-30 mins, until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool cakes in pans for 10 mins. Remove from pans, cool completely on wire racks.
  8. Whisk lemon juice and powdered sugar for glaze. Drizzle over cooled cakes.
  9. Slice and serve your delicious Lemon Drizzle Cake!
