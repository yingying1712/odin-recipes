Toasty Pokémon Cookies

Descriptions:

Capture the fun and flavor with these delightful Pokémon Cookies!

These sugar cookies are SOFT and BUTTERY

Ingredients:

  • Flour: 3 cups
  • Powder:1 teaspoon
  • Baking Soda: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Softened Butter: 1 cup
  • Sugar: 2 cups
  • Egg: 1 large egg
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon
  • Milk: 2 tablespoons (optional, for added moisture)

- Simple Steps to Make Pokémon Cookies -

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Mix in egg and vanilla (and milk if using).
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet, mixing until dough forms.
  5. Wrap dough in plastic and chill for 1 hour.
  6. Roll dough to 1/4-inch thickness on floured surface. Cut into shapes.
  7. Place cookies on sheets and bake 8-10 min until edges are lightly golden.
  8. Cool on racks. Decorate with icing and sprinkles.
  9. Serve and enjoy your homemade Pokémon cookies!
