Snuggle Sandwich

Descriptions:

A delightful hug of flavours tucked between slices of happiness!

Ingredients:

  • Bread: 2 slices
  • Cheese:1 slice
  • Tomato: 2 slices
  • Lettuce Leaves: 2 pieces
  • Ham: 1 slice

- Simple Steps to Make Snuggle Sandwich -

  1. Place two slices of bread on a clean surface.
  2. Place lettuce leaves and sliced tomatoes on one slice of bread.
  3. Add slices of ham on top of the lettuce and sliced tomatoes.
  4. Add slices of cheese on top of the ham.
  5. Place the other slice of bread on top to close the sandwich.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious homemade Snuggle Sandwich!
